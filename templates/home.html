Our Concept & aims

Our Emotion-Based Music Recommendation System is designed to enhance the music-listening experience by understanding and responding to users' emotional states. The core concept is to use emotion detection technology to analyze users' facial expressions or mood inputs, accurately identifying feelings such as happiness, sadness, excitement, calmness, and more. Based on these emotions, the system recommends personalized music that resonates with the user’s current mood, providing a deeply personalized and supportive experience.
Looking to the future, we aim to implement a "Creative Mode" that allows users to showcase their musical talents through the app.
